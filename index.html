Examples Of My Work

As you can probably tell, I'm hyped to get underway in Computer Science. The thing I look forward to most is developing the ability to understand programming problems innately, and then sharing my knowledge with someone in need of help. I think that many of the world's problems, such as its inefficient logistical system for the distribution of resources, can be solved with Computer Science. If people like my classmates and myself step up to programming for the good of the world, I know we can do great things.
