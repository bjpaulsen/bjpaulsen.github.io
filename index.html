Queer Time
This game is about an idea in queer theory called "Queer Time." It's the idea that lots of queer people experience the future differently, because of a different relationship with marriage, love, and having kids. The game will be about traveling through Queer Time, from the perspective of a kid who's figuring out their idea of the future.
Core Gameplay:
  • Timed Levels & Warped Spacetime
    • Complete levels in limited time.
    • Find warps in spacetime that change how time moves (e.g. pause time, speed up time, slow time, turn back time).
    • Find items that replenish used time.
  • Platformer Progression
    • Learn to use movement skills to traverse increasingly difficult and unexpected terrain.
  • Bosses
    • Outrun and outsmart monsters that represent a future that is forced on you.
  • Nonlinear Upgrades
    • Augment your play with new movement mechanics or new rules of play.
    • Each augment has pros and cons that create interesting decisions.
Fling Fleet
Fling Fleet is about competing with your friends in a strategic space battle. It's designed to have a multitude of viable strategies, factions, and ships to choose from. The photo you see above was version 1.0 written in Java using Processing. A prototype of Version 2.0 is shown in the video above, made in C# and Unity.
Core Gameplay:
  • Turn-Based Strategy
    • Command all your ships directly on each turn.
    • Collect Scrap to build a bigger fleet.
  • Flinging Fun
    • Catapult your ships across the stars with a unique movement mechanic.
  • Factions
    • Choose from 4 factions with completely different playstyles and ships at their disposal.
Stomp Out
Stomp out is a vertical fighting game and a race to the top. Bounce up the platforms and STOMP your friends out! An early prototype of movement is shown above. The game will be built in Unity and C#.
Core Gameplay:
  • Timing is Key
    • Use a powerful downward stomp to bounce off of platforms.
    • Use the same ability to battle your friends downward.
  • Unlock Unique Abilities
    • Though it starts out simple, players can unlock powers of levitation, super-stomps, dodges, and sideways-stomps!
