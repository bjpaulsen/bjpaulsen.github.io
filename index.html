ABOUT ME

Hello, I’m Benjamin! I am a sophomore studying Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz.





I’m interested in cooperating with other engineers to build our knowledge and learn to develop effective, inclusive teams. That’s why I became a tutor. I’m a good listener and communicator, I love to code, and I’m a patient teacher.




I believe that people working together can become better and create more than they can alone. If we truly listen to one another, and utilize our diverse set of experiences as students and engineers, I know we can create a welcoming and empowering place of learning. I’m truly excited to contribute to the field of computer science, and I think that my enthusiasm is one of my greatest strengths.




I have experience serving the public, from children and teens, to adults and seniors. I have worked at a local library, helped organize and run two blood drives, fed our community at Food Not Bombs, and cared for animals at the Santa Cruz SPCA. For more on what I can contribute, be sure to read my resume. I look forward to working with you!
