What have I accomplished?

As you can probably tell, I'm hyped to get underway in AP Computer Science. The thing I look forward to most is developing the ability to understand programming problems innately, and then sharing my knowledge with someone in need of help. I think that many of the world's problems, such as its inefficient logistical system for the distribution of resources, can be solved with Computer Science.
